# My AI/ML Internship at NIELIT: What I Learned

From June to August 2024, I interned at NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology) in Chandigarh as an AI/ML Summer Trainee. It was an intense, rewarding three months.

## What I Worked On

### Predictive Modeling
I built regression and classification models using scikit-learn, working through the full ML pipeline: data collection, preprocessing, model training, and evaluation.

### Data Preprocessing
Real-world data is messy. I handled missing values, outliers, and categorical encoding daily. Learning to make these decisions thoughtfully—rather than just filling with means or dropping rows—was one of the most valuable parts of the internship.

### NLP Techniques
I got hands-on time with text data: tokenization, TF-IDF vectorization, and basic sentiment analysis. NLP felt like magic the first time a model correctly classified a piece of text it had never seen.

### AI/GenAI Patterns
Toward the end of the internship, I explored modern AI patterns including retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), which opened my eyes to how production AI systems actually work beyond just the model.

## Tools & Libraries

- **NumPy & Pandas** – data wrangling
- **Matplotlib** – visualization
- **scikit-learn** – modeling
- **FastAPI** – serving predictions

## What's Next

The internship confirmed that AI Engineering is the path I want to pursue. I'm now actively working on projects that combine ML with proper software engineering practices, aiming for a role where I can build AI systems that solve real problems.
